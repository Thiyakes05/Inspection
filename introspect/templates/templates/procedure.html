Inspection Procedure

Preparation Requirements

Please ensure you have the following instruments ready for the inspection:

  • A functioning camera (for live streaming and capturing images)
  • Stable internet connection (to ensure smooth data transfer)
  • Any necessary measurement tools (e.g., tape measure, gauges)
  • Access to relevant documents or files related to the inspection

Please follow the steps below to complete your inspection:

  1. Initiating the Inspection: Click the “Start Inspection” button to begin the process.
  2. Data Submission: Provide all necessary information as prompted, including images, documents, or specific parameters.
  3. Review and Confirmation: Review the entered information and confirm that all details are correct.
  4. Inspection Process: Allow the AI system to process your data. Do not navigate away from the page during this process.
  5. Receiving Results: After completion, you will receive a detailed report. Review the findings carefully.
  6. Post-Inspection Actions: Implement any necessary actions based on the results, and reach out to our support for clarification if needed.
